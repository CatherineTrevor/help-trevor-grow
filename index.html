Welcome to Help Trevor Grow!

This is Trevor! He needs your help to grow from an Egg, into a Caterpillar and then a Butterfly!

Pick the correct fruit to feed Trevor and help him grow, but you need to be quick as the fruit will disappear!

How to play

This free-to-play memory game is designed to help young children, between approx. 3 and 5 years old, learn and remember basic fruits and numbers up to 10. The game has three levels:

Egg

The simplest level, designed for approx. 3 years old.

Caterpillar

A bit harder, designed for approx 4 years old.

Butterfly

The hardest level, designed for approx. 5 years old.

These ages are just a guidance on the level of difficulty involved, they are not an indication of the level a child "should" be. This game is designed to be fun!

The fruit that Trevor wants to eat is shown, with three options below. Select the correct option to gain a point and start a new turn. Be careful though, as the fruit will disappear...

Select a level from the options above, or go straight into the game, starting at Egg level. Don't forget to select a color theme!
