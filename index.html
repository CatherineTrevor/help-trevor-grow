Welcome to Help Trevor Grow!


This free-to-play memory game is designed to help young children, between approx. 3 and 5 years old, learn and remember basic fruits and numbers up to 10!


Pick the correct fruit to feed Trevor and help him grow from an Egg, into a Caterpillar and then a Butterfly!

The game has three levels:

Egg - the simplest level, designed for approx. 3 years old.

Caterpillar - a bit harder, designed for approx 4 years old.

Butterfly - the hardest level, designed for approx. 5 years old.

These ages are just a guidance on the level of difficult involved, they are not an indication of the level a child "should" be. This game is designed to be fun!
