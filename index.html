Welcome to Help Trevor Grow!


This is Trevor! He needs your help to grow from an Egg, into a Caterpillar and then a Butterfly!


Pick the correct fruit to feed Trevor and help him grow, but you need to be quick as the fruit will disappear!


About the game

This free-to-play memory game is designed to help young children, between approx. 3 and 5 years old, learn and remember basic fruits and numbers up to 10.

The game has three levels:

Egg - the simplest level, designed for approx. 3 years old.

Caterpillar - a bit harder, designed for approx 4 years old.

Butterfly - the hardest level, designed for approx. 5 years old.


These ages are just a guidance on the level of difficulty involved, they are not an indication of the level a child "should" be. This game is designed to be fun!

The fruit that Trevor wants to eat is shown, with three options below. Select the correct option to gain a point and start a new turn. Be careful though, as the fruit may disappear...

From the playing page, select the level to play, as well as a color theme for the page!
